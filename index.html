About me

Solving problems
that matter.

I'm a Computer Science & Data Analytics undergraduate at IIT Patna focused on building systems that turn data into meaningful insights.

My interests sit at the intersection of software engineering, data analysis, and machine learning — where clean code meets real-world decision making. I enjoy designing tools, dashboards, and analytical systems that help people understand complex data clearly.

Recently I've been working on data visualization projects, analytical dashboards, and small full-stack tools, while continuing to strengthen my foundations in algorithms, systems, and machine learning.

I’m naturally curious about how technology works at a deeper level. I enjoy learning new tools, experimenting with emerging technologies, and continuously expanding my technical understanding through hands-on projects.

Outside of coding I enjoy cricket, badminton, and exploring topics like economics, environmental data, and global systems. I'm also interested in learning about world languages and cultures, and I enjoy studying languages such as Japanese and Spanish to better understand different societies and perspectives.
