how it works

How your data is processed

The enquiry form is powered by Formspree, a third-party form handling service. When you submit the form:

  • Your submission is sent securely to Formspree's servers
  • Formspree forwards it to my personal email inbox
  • I read your message and may reply to your email directly

Formspree has its own privacy policy which you can read at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

data usage

How I use your data

Your submitted information is used only to:

I do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third party beyond Formspree's processing described above.
